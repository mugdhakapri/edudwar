This Year...

For The Year 2023, we have some amazing competitive exams:


1. Indian National Mathematical Olympiad (INMO): A mathematics competition held annually in India, it is a stepping stone for Indian students to participate in international mathematical Olympiads.
2. Indian National Physics Olympiad (INPhO): This olympiad identifies and nurtures young talents in physics.

3. Indian National Chemistry Olympiad (INChO): Similar to INPhO, INChO promotes excellence in chemistry among students.

4. Indian National Biology Olympiad (INBO): A biology competition to encourage students to explore the field of biology.

5. Astronomy Olympiad Program: It includes the Indian National Astronomy Olympiad (INAO), Indian Astronomy Olympiad (IAO), and the International Olympiad on Astronomy and Astrophysics (IOAA).

6. Indian Computing Olympiad (ICO): Conducted by the Indian Association for Research in Computing Science (IARCS), it is a gateway to international computing olympiads.

7. Homi Bhabha Balvaidnyanik Spardha: Named after Dr. Homi Bhabha, this competition encourages students to take an interest in science.

8. National Cyber Olympiad (NCO): Focused on computer knowledge and IT skills.

9. Green Olympiad: Conducted by TERI (The Energy and Resources Institute), it focuses on environmental issues.

10. Junior Science Olympiad (JSO): A stepping stone for the International Junior Science Olympiad (IJSO).

11. National Science Olympiad (NSO): Tests the scientific aptitude of students.

12. Robotics Olympiad: Various robotics competitions are organized across India to promote STEM and robotics education.
